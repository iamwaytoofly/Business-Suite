Find the Perfect Business Bank Account

A business bank account is essential for separating your personal and business finances. We'll help you find the right account and guide you through the opening process.

Why You Need a Business Bank Account

  • Legal Protection: Keeps your personal assets separate from your business
  • Professional Image: Clients can make payments to your business name
  • Tax Simplification: Makes tracking business expenses and income easier
  • Business Credit: Helps establish and build your business credit history

What You'll Need to Open an Account

  • EIN (Employer Identification Number)
  • Business formation documents (Articles of Organization, etc.)
  • Business license (if required for your industry)
  • Personal identification

Account Opening Guide

Follow these steps to open your business bank account successfully.

Business Bank Account Opening Guide

Selected Bank: First National Bank

Account Type: Basic Business Checking

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Gather all required documents

    Make sure you have all the necessary documents ready before visiting the branch. This includes your EIN, business formation documents, identification, and any other required paperwork.

  2. Visit the branch or attend your scheduled appointment

    Arrive at the branch with all your documents. If you've scheduled an appointment, you'll meet with a business banking specialist who will guide you through the process.

  3. Complete the account application

    You'll need to fill out an account application form. The bank representative will help you with this process and answer any questions you may have.

  4. Provide your initial deposit

    Most banks require an initial deposit to open your account. Be prepared to make this deposit via cash, check, or transfer from another account.

  5. Set up online banking

    Once your account is open, you'll want to set up online banking access. This will allow you to manage your account, make transfers, and pay bills online.

  6. Order checks and debit cards

    If needed, you can order business checks and debit cards for your account. These typically arrive within 7-10 business days.
