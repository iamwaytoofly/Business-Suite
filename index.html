How It Works

  1. Form Your LLC - Create your business entity with proper legal protection
  2. Get Your EIN - Obtain your tax ID number from the IRS
  3. Open a Bank Account - Separate your business and personal finances
  4. Secure Licenses & Permits - Ensure you're legally allowed to operate

We recommend completing these steps in order, but you can start with any step that meets your current needs.
