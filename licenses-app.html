Why Business Licenses Matter

Operating without proper licenses and permits can result in:

  • Fines and penalties that can cost thousands of dollars
  • Business closure until you obtain the required licenses
  • Legal liability for operating an unauthorized business
  • Difficulty getting business loans or insurance coverage

How This Tool Works

We'll ask you a few simple questions about:

  • Your business location (state, county, city)
  • Your industry and specific business activities
  • Special circumstances that might require additional permits

Then we'll provide you with a customized list of all the licenses and permits you need, along with instructions on how to obtain them.

Federal

Employer Identification Number (EIN)

+

An Employer Identification Number (EIN) is a unique nine-digit number assigned by the IRS to identify your business for tax purposes. It's like a Social Security Number for your business.

Issuing Agency

Internal Revenue Service (IRS)

Fee

Free

Processing Time

Immediate (online) or 4-5 weeks (mail)

Renewal

Not required

Requirements

  • Legal business name
  • Business address
  • Responsible party's Social Security Number
Federal

Alcohol Beverage License

+

Required for businesses that manufacture, wholesale, import, or sell alcoholic beverages. This federal permit is required in addition to any state and local alcohol licenses.

Issuing Agency

Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB)

Fee

$100 - $1,000 (varies by type)

Processing Time

90-120 days

Renewal

Annual

Requirements

  • Business formation documents
  • Floor plan of premises
  • Background check
  • Proof of financial responsibility
State

Business License

+

A general business license required for most businesses operating in the state. This license registers your business with the state tax authority.

Issuing Agency

State Department of Revenue

Fee

$90

Processing Time

10-14 business days

Renewal

Annual

Requirements

  • Business name and address
  • Business structure information
  • Federal EIN
  • Description of business activities
